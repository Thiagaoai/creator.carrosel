## Carousel Structure Rules
- **Slide 1 (hook):** Sensory and immediate. Ex: "You have never had cheese bread like this." or "That sound when you pull it apart — that is the one."
- **Middle slides:** One fact, recipe tip, pairing, or story per slide. Keep it warm and specific.
- **Last slide (CTA):** Invitation-style. Ex: "Order yours today." or "Tell us how you eat yours — with coffee or straight from the bag?"
- Captions should make the reader hungry or nostalgic in under 10 words per slide.

## Tone Examples
- Good: "Three ingredients. One perfect bite. That is pão de queijo."
- Good: "The outside is crispy. The inside is soft and chewy. There is nothing like it."
- Bad: "Cheesebread offers delicious Brazilian cheese bread for all occasions!"
